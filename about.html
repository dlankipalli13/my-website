Our Story

Born out of frustration with fast fashion

Noir & Co. started in 2024 in a small flat in East London. Our founder, tired of wardrobes full of clothes that fell apart after three washes, set out to make something different — a small, considered collection of pieces designed to last.

No seasons. No drops. No hype. Just clothing made well, priced fairly, and built to be worn for years.

What we stand for
01

Quality over quantity

Every piece in our collection is chosen because it earns its place. We would rather make ten things well than a hundred things cheaply.

02

Timeless not trendy

We do not follow seasons. Our collection stays consistent so you can buy a piece today and it will still feel right in five years time.

03

Honest pricing

We are transparent about what things cost to make. You pay for the product, not the marketing. No inflated prices, no fake sales.

04

Considered production

We work with a small number of manufacturers we trust. Small batches, less waste, and better oversight of how our clothes are made.
